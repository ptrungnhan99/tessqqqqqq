mangoes, passion fruits & apples

We have words for almost everything. The sound of wind in the trees is known as ‘psithurism’. ‘Sprezzatura’ means putting loads of effort into making it look like you haven't put any effort in. Then there’s ‘defenestration’, which describes the act of throwing someone out of a window. But there are still a few words we don’t have yet. Like a collective term for nephews and nieces or a decent word to describe how tasty this tropical blend of creamy mango, fragrant passion fruit and zesty orange really is. If you manage to find one, please do send it in. It might save us from being ‘defenestrated’ in our end of year review.

mangoes, passion fruits & apples
